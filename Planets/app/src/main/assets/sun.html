The Sun, also referred to as “Sol” , is the star at the center of the solar system. Sun’s mass accounts for some 99.86% of the total mass of the solar system.


Composition

Roughly three-quarters of the Sun’s mass is
hydrogen, with the rest mostly heliium.
Only 1.69% of the Sun ( which is still 5 628 times
the mass of the Earth ) is made up of heavier
elements like cvarbon, iron, neon and oxygen.


Distance

The mean distance of the Sun from the Earth
is approximately 149.6 million kilometers
( 1 AU ). On average, it takes light from the Sun
about 8 minutes and 19 seconds to reach the
Earth.


Galaxy

Once thought to be a relatively insignificant
star, the Sun is now considered to be brighter
than about 85% of the stars in the Milky Way
galaxy. It is located about two thirds of the way
out from the center of the Milky Way.
